Tickets

We have a wide range of different packages for tickets. These include VIP, Standing and Seating. Please check the infomation below for what you get with each ticket and what extras are included.

 

VIP - £100, you get: A full 3 course, sit down meal - Entry for the whole festival on both stages - Reserved seats on both stages - free golf buggy shuttle to where you want to go - free t-shirt and glow sticks - backstage passes to meet all the acts

Seating - £50, you get: Entry to both stages with access to the seating area - Free £5 voucher for food outlets* - glow sticks included

Standing - £35, you get: Entry to both standing areas of the festival - Free £5 voucher for food outlets* - glow sticks included

Budget - £20, If you only want to see one stage then there's no need to buy a full price ticket, buy a budget one. You get: Standing access to one stage of your choice and a 25% off voucher for food*.
