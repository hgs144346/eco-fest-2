Eco Fest 2019 is here!

We are back and better than ever before!

 

Last year and this year Eco Fest was a HUGE success! With over 20,000 vistiors through the gates, we saw astronomical amount of supporters of the music and also the values that we support. We really liked the reaction that we got on social media and have decided to go ahead for 2019. With more acts than ever, Eco Fest is going to be where it's at.

With artists such as DJ John Alfred and drumming marvels such as Solar Drum and indie band Everyones Enviroment. We also have singers such as Sia and Jess Glyne. Check the 'line-up' for more info on the acts.

For infonmation on Tickets and purchasing tickets, please see the 'Tickets' page. Our ticket prices start at just £35 for a standing ticket and all the profit made goes towards the protection of wildlife fund and the national recycling fund. This money pays for vital research towards renweable energy and the protection of wildlife from the mess that WE have caused. See the 'Recycling Objects' page for more info on this.
